INTRODUCTION                                    *vim-flip-comparands*

Vim Flip Comparison is a plugin that allows you to flip two comparands
around a comparison operator.

USAGE                                           *vim-flip-comparands-mappings*

Place the cursor over a comparison operator and call :FlipC
